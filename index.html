How Our Age Calculator Works

Our age calculator computes your age based on your date of birth and the current date. It accurately determines the time that has passed, breaking it down into years, months, and days. This provides a more precise measure of age than just subtracting the birth year from the current year. It's a perfect tool for filling out applications, planning events, or just satisfying your curiosity!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator is highly accurate as it accounts for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you your precise age.

Is my data saved?

No. We respect your privacy. All calculations are done directly in your browser. We do not store any personal information like your date of birth.

Can I calculate the age of a past or future event?

Yes, while it's designed for birth dates, you can use it to find the duration between any two dates. Just enter the start date in the input fields and compare it to the current date.
